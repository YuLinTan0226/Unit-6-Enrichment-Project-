What They Buy

Taiwan needs these to keep their factories humming:

  • Energy - Oil, gas, coal (they import 98% of their energy!)
  • Factory Machines - High-tech equipment
  • Cars & Parts - For getting around the island
  • Medicine - Keeping everyone healthy
  • Food - Soybeans, corn, beef
